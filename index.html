What is Snap Them All ?

Snap Them All is a game where your goal is to take snaps of almost everything. Our algorithm gives you back a score reprensenting how much your snap helps us. Get the best score and become the world’s best ‘snapper’.

What is a good snap ?

A good snap is unique. This means you should think about the angle, the location, the background, etc..  Our algorithm is looking for diversity. Be yourself, be creative.

Who’s behind it ?

Snap Them All is a bachelor project where one of the goal was to design a crowdsourcing mobile app. The project was implemented by Grégoire Clément, student at EPFL.
